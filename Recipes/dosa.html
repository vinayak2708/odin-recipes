Dosa

The Dosa is a staple breakfast in many Southern Indian Households.

Made out of Rice, it is usually had with Sambhar and Coconut Chutney

Recipe

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Water (as required)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter using water as needed.
  3. Transfer the batter to a bowl, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8–10 hours.
  4. Heat a flat pan or tawa and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter onto the tawa and spread it evenly in a circular motion.
  6. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  7. Fold the dosa and serve hot with chutney or sambar.
